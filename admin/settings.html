🎮 Game Win Ratio (RTP)
Sets the house payout ratio for every game on the platform (Aviator, Chicken Road, and any future game).
• Higher % → players win more often, house earns less.
• Lower % → house earns more.
Allowed range 50–99%. Takes effect on new rounds within seconds — no restart needed.
Caps the payout a player can win in a single round of any game — protects the house from one lucky max-bet round. 0 = no cap. The amount is in your base units (same number across PKR/INR/USD).
